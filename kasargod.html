BEKAL FORT-Built almost 300 years ago, Bekal Fort is considered as one of the largest and most preserved forts in Kerala.
HOSDURG FORT-Hosdurg Fort near Kanhangad is renowned for its chain of forts built by Somashekara Nayak of Ikkeri Dynasty.
THONIKADAVU-Thonikadavu in Kundamkuzy in Kasaragod District is known for its virgin forest and mist clad meadows. This place is just few kilometers away from the Historical Bekal Fort
